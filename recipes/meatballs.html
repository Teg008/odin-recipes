Meatballs

Description

An easy recipe for homemade pan fried meatballs. Only a few ingridients required, great for a quick meal that will satiate the entire family.

Ingridients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Tomato sauce of your choosing
  • Ritz crackers
  • Parsley
  • Honey
  • Grape Jelly
  • Oregano
  • Your choice of pasta

Steps

  1. Crush Ritz crackers in a plastic bag
  2. Combine all ingridients in bowl except tomato sauce
  3. Mix ground beef with seasonings until distributed throughout
  4. Place large frying pan on stove over medium heat and add a layer of tomato sauce to pan
  5. Arrange meatballs in the pan snugly
  6. Cover meatballs with remaining sauce and put lid on the frying pan
  7. Cook meatballs over medium heat for 20 minutes
  8. Serve with your choice of pasta
