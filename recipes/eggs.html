Hard-Boiled Eggs

Descripton

Hard boiled eggs aree a great way to obtain much needed protein and satiation. They are easy to make in a pinch and portable to boot.

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill medium saucepan with water and place eggs inside
  2. Turn stove to high, cover pan and wait for water to boil
  3. Once water is boiling turn stove off and let eggs cook for 15 minutes
  4. Take pan off stove empty water and refill with cold water
  5. Keep cold water running incase eggs are hit while de-shelling
  6. Hit eggs on inside of sink and peel away the shell
  7. Enjoy eggs with hot sauce
