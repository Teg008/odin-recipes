Chilli

Description

This recipe will showcase the infamous chilli from the American television show The Office. Kevin Malone is an excellent chilli chef and known for his world famous Malone Chilli.

In this section I will go over the step by step process of making the world famous Malone chilli. It will be an arduous journey, but well worth the time and effort put in when the final product is presented.

Ingridients

  • Ground Beef 1 pound
  • Bell Peppers
  • Banana Peppers
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Sweet Onions
  • Chilli Powder
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Kidney Beans
  • 1 Can diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cut all vegetables to a size of your liking
  2. Turn stove to medium and brown ground beef in large cooking pot
  3. Once browned, drain fat from ground beef and clean the pot
  4. Return ground beef to the pan add vegetables and turn to low heat
  5. Let simmer with cover on until vegetables turn brighter in color
  6. Add Kidney Beans
